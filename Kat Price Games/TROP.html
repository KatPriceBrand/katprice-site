About the Game

The Rise of Price is an immersive visual novel that weaves two unique storylines into one compelling narrative. Your choices shape the story, leading to two distinct paths in the final act. Every decision matters as you uncover different perspectives and outcomes.

Features:

  • two interconnected storylines
  • Meaningful choices that affect the story
  • Multiple endings to discover
  • Original artwork and soundtrack

Technical Details & Rights

The Rise of Price was developed using Ren'Py, a visual novel engine. All game assets, including artwork, music, and story content, are original works created by Kat Price Games. All rights reserved.
